FAQs

What is Frontend Mentor, and how will it help me?

Frontend Mentor is a platform that helps you improve your coding skills by providing real-world projects.

Is Frontend Mentor free?

Yes, Frontend Mentor offers free challenges. There are also premium challenges available for a fee.

Can I use Frontend Mentor projects in my portfolio?

Yes, you can showcase the projects you build on Frontend Mentor in your portfolio.

How can I get help if I'm stuck on a Frontend Mentor challenge?

You can seek help from the community forums or read the solutions provided by other users.
